Bose loudspeaker recall and safety cable installation

Applies to:

     * Freespace_DS40

   Bose is recalling all FreeSpace DS 40F in-ceiling/flush-mounted
   loudspeakers manufactured prior to 13 August 2018. Additionally, Bose
   is requiring the installation of safety cables for certain Bose
   loudspeakers, including DS 40F, that are installed in commercial
   cooking environments.

   For more information, contact your Bose dealer. If you are unsure who
   your dealer is, visit our Global sales offices page.

   If you are a direct Bose dealer, please go to Bose BMS safety for more
   information.
