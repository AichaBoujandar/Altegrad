Need more than one rating per page?
         ______________________________________________________________

       Simply assign a unique string as the rating title for every rating
       on your site. For example you can use the following code for the
       first rating:
<div class="rw-ui-container" data-title="my awesome rating"></div>
       for the second rating use:
<div class="rw-ui-container" data-title="another great rating"></div>
       And so on.
       Note: If you assign the same ID to different items, the same rating
       would be rendered.
       If you use server-side scriptiong (e.g. PHP, Ruby, ASP.NET), and
       your rated items have a unique ID, simply use:

          +
