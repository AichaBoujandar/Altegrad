   (BUTTON) Make a donation to MSRI

   Use our secure online giving portal (via Classy) to make a gift to
   MSRI. This button will open the donation form in a new browser window.

   For donations made via postal mail:

   Mathematical Sciences Research Institute
   Attention: Development
   17 Gauss Way
   Berkeley CA 94720

   If you would prefer to make a donation to MSRI using a donor advised
   fund, a gift of stock, or a wire transfer, please contact Annie Averitt
   at aaveritt@msri.org or by phone at (510) 643-6056.
   Ways to Support MSRI
   Individuals can make a significant impact on the life of the Institute,
   on mathematics, and on scientific research by contributing to MSRI.
   Learn more about annual gifts, endowment gifts, and planned gifts.
   Museion Society

   The Museion Society comprise MSRI donors at the highest levels of
   support, with annual gifts beginning at $5,040. For more information,
   contact Annie Averitt, Director of Advancement and External Relations,
   at aaveritt@msri.org.
   Academic Sponsors
   Our Academic Sponsors.
   Publisher Sponsors
   MSRI is fortunate to have the support of a number of publishers who
   make their publications and journals, both print and on line, available
   for use in the Austine McDonnell Hearst Library.

   A list of Foundation supporters.
