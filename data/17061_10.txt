   Abstract : Let $\mathcal{X}=\{X_1,\ldots X_n\}\subset \mathbb{R}^d$ be
   a random sample of observations drawn with a probability distribution
   supported on $S$ satisfying that both $S$ and $\overline{S^c}$ are
   $r_0$-convex ($r_0>0$). In this paper we propose an estimator of the
   medial axis of $S$ based on the $\lambda$-medial axis and the
   $r$-convex hull. Its convergence rate is derived. An heuristic to tune
   the parameters of the estimator is given and a small simulation study
   is performed.
