   Logan Bartholomew, Christianne Chua and Maggie Steiner



   Junior Logan Bartholomew (Chemistry), second-year Christianne Chua
   (Biomedical Engineering) and junior Maggie Steiner (Applied
   Mathematics) have been named 2019 Goldwater Scholars, a prestigious
   recognition for undergraduate students pursuing careers in the natural
   sciences, engineering and mathematics. These students have previously
   pursued research projects with faculty members and will use their
   awards to continue to make a positive impact on the world around them.
